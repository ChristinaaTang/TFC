Contracts

Brighten up your space

Working together, we will produce bespoke designs for a place of your choosing; be it for business environments, such as an office, or personal spaces, such as your home. We will also handle the delivery and installation, so there is no need to worry about setting up the designs once you’ve decided on them.

Business contracts

Whether you need to fill an office space or restaurant, we will offer catered services in order to fulfil your needs. A little bit of luxury goes a long way, and floral arrangements are perfect to lift the mood. You can tell us exactly what you want, or you can leave it to us and our expert opinion to integrate the perfect floral arrangements into your place of work.

Home contracts

Fresh flowers help make a house a home. Wow your guests with gorgeous floral arrangements that compliment the atmosphere and ambience of your house. To help us envision the space you’re looking to create, we’re more than happy to carry out a complimentary consultation in your home. Alternatively, we can have a chat over the phone or by email.

Our contractual services offer:

  • Flexible contracts

    All contracts can be negotiated to decide on the length of the contract, and whether maintenance is required. Maintenance may include the watering and general upkeep of displays to keep them looking fresh and stunning.

  • Bespoke designs

    We encourage all clients to tell us what you like or dislike, so the perfect arrangements can be created. Details such as vases and containers can be discussed so that the designs fit right in with the environment.

  • Delivery and Installation

    Leave the delivery and installation to us. You won’t have to worry about collecting the floral arrangements and then setting up the arrangements. To make the process as easy as possible, we can do this for you.
