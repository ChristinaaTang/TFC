Sympathy

Send your condolences

Select from a wide range of sympathy flowers to give your loved ones your condelences. Choose your desired flowers and style of arrangement, such as wreaths or coffin sprays, and let us handle the rest. Rest assured that your arrangements will arrive in a timely manner, with your kind wishes.

Totteridge Flower Company recommends using these flowers in sympathy arrangements

  • Lillies

    Most commonly associated with funeral services, lillies symbolise majesty and purity. Lillies are available in white.

  • Roses

    Roses convey feelings of respect and love, and including a single rose in the arrangement expresses enduring love for the deceased. Roses are available in white, pink, yellow and red.

  • Carnations

    A popular choice for sympathy arrangements, carnations represent remembrance and admiration. Carnations are available in red, pink and white.

  • Gladioli

    Gladioli are elegant and a classifc for traditional funeral services. They represent sincerity and integrity, and are avaialble in a range of colours such as white, pink, purple and orange.
