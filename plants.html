Indoor, Outdoor, and Seasonal Plants

Indoor and Outdoor Perennials

Succulents and cactus are brilliant for those who are looking for stylish, yet low maintenance plants. Their structured leaves bring a modern feel to the home, and combined with a terrarium, look chic and attractive. Orchids are well known for looking elegant and exotic. With care, they can be enjoyed throughout the year, flowering multiple times. Orchids also come in a variety of colours, so they can be matched to the colour scheme of your home, some popular colours include pink, white and yellow.

Spring and Summer Plants

Enjoy the glorious spring time atmosphere from indoors. The British springtime is notoriously wet and grey, but there is no better way to brighten up the mood than with bright and cheerful indoor plants. Indoor plants that are best enjoyed during the spring time include hyacinths, narcissi, daffodils. Summer brings in a brighter arrangement of indoor and outdoor plants, including Sunflowers, which are best enjoyed outdoors. Sunflowers are low maintenance and are always a popular choice with children. It is important that flowers are watered more frequently during the summer to the rise in temperature. Some indoor plants can also be transferred outside to enjoy the summer sun, such as jasmines and bougainvillea.

Autumn and Winter Plants

As the months get cooler, the new season introduces a plethora of different plant life. Some popular choices to include in planted arrangements are poinsettia. Poinsettia are synonymous with the festive season, and help add those finishing touches to your decorations. Silver leaf is also a popular choice, as the frosted leaves compliment the chilly winter air. Silver leaf is hardy, and is therefore able to endure temperamental British winters.
